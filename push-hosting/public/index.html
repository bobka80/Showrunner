Install Showrunner

Add to your home screen for schedule access and shift alerts — no browser bar, works like an app.

iPhone
1. Tap Share (square with arrow) at the bottom of Safari
2. Tap Add to Home Screen
3. Tap Add — then open Showrunner from your home screen
Android
1. Tap the menu ⋮ in Chrome (or use Install below)
2. Tap Add to Home screen or Install app
3. Open Showrunner from your home screen
